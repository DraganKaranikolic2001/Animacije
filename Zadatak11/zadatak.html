Game Rules - Double or Nothing: Red or Black

-After each win in the base game, the player gets a chance to double the winnings through the "Red or Black" mini-game.

-The player chooses whether the card will be red (hearts/diamonds) or black (spades/clubs).

-If the guess is correct, the winnings are doubled and the player can try again.

-If the guess is wrong, the player loses the entire winnings from the previous game.

-A maximum of 5 successful doubling attempts is allowed per single win.

-The player can choose to stop and keep the current winnings at any time.

Play responsibly. One wrong guess means losing the entire winnings.
