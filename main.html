Game rules

All pays are left to right, beginning with the leftmost reel, except scatters symbols. Scatter symbols pay at any position on screen and scatter symbols pay at anywhere on 1st, 3rd and 5th reels. Highest payline win only paid. The total bet in a slot game is calculated by multiplying the number of active paylines by the bet per line. In autoplay mode, the slot game will automatically collect any winnings you earn on each spin, and add them to your total balance. If the gamble feature interrupts the autoplay in a slot game, the autoplay will stop and the game will prompt you to play the gamble feature.
